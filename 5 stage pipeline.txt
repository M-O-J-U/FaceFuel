FaceFuel is a five-stage pipeline that estimates nutritional deficiency
probabilities from a selfie in 58.3 ms. Combining YOLOv8m lesion detection,
region-aware DINOv2 ViT-S/14 feature extraction across eight facial zones,
and Bayesian inference, the system achieves mean F1 = 0.677 across eleven
skin feature classes without blood tests or clinical equipment.
